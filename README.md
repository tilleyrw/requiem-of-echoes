About the Book

Eldorann Dreamshadow is ash'dorei: a child of light born among the children of night, carrying the dual divine nature of Cenarius and Elune in a body that betrays every feeling she has. Her skin shifts through the spectrum of what she cannot hide — red for rage, green for calm, violet for the transcendence she does not yet understand. For sixteen years she has been told she is broken and needs fixing. Then Tharae Wildstalker finds her, reads her correctly for the first time, and reframes the question: not broken, only unmatched to her teachers.

What follows is an apprenticeship that becomes a partnership that becomes a love, threaded through the long lunar arc of a story told in twelve phases — new moon to lunar eclipse. There is the hunt that proves her worth. The journey to Lor'danel. The sister-warriors at Astranaar. And the Blood Moon at Splintertree, where the Little Moon is shattered and everything Eldorann has built must be carried forward in a form she did not choose.

This is psychological fantasy in the Donaldson register — long sentences and short, characters under simultaneous pressure from two directions, no clean resolutions. Beneath the magic lies real consciousness research, authentic tantric practice, and trauma honestly examined. Because in the end, echoes are not what we have forgotten. Echoes are the lessons we have avoided.

The Series

Requiem of Echoes is planned as five books, each shaped by a mythological descent:

Echoes of a Shattered Moon — Eldorann's first arc (complete)

Echoes of Mortality — Inanna's descent (in production)

Echoes of Conflict — Persephone

Echoes of Peace — Orpheus

Echoes of Being — Osiris

Soundtrack
A chapter-by-chapter album in the style of Amy Lee and Evanescence is being produced through Suno. Songs are linked from their corresponding chapter author's notes on Royal Road.

About the Author

Robert Tilley writes fantasy that integrates consciousness research, tantric practice, and trauma-informed storytelling into the World of Warcraft setting. Requiem of Echoes has been in development since approximately 2015.
