# Requiem-of-Echoes
Warcraft Fan-Fiction Series

Five-Book Structure Book 1: Echoes of a Shattered Moon - Follows relationship with Tharae through death and resurrection 

Book 2: Echoes of Mortality - Grapples with Forsaken identity 

Book 3: Echoes of Conflict - Rekindling discovery causes rifts 

Book 4: Echoes of Peace - Tensions ease as more undergo Rekindling 

Book 5: Echoes of Being - Explores existential questions raised by the process
